About Me

Welcome! I am Giulio Binosi, a researcher specializing in hypercomplex analysis, harmonic analysis, slice analysis and complex and differential geometry. I work primarily with quaternionic and Clifford algebras.

Research Interests

  • Hypercomplex Analysis
  • Harmonic Analysis
  • Slice Analysis
  • Complex Geometry
  • Differential Geometry
