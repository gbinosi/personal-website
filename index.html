About Me

Welcome! I am Giulio Binosi, a researcher specializing in hypercomplex analysis, harmonic analysis, slice analysis, Dunkl analysis and complex and differential geometry. I work primarily with quaternionic and Clifford algebras. I completeed my PhD at Trento university, now I am a Postdoc at Florence university with INdAM's project SUNRISE.
