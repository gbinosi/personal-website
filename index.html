About Me

Welcome! I am Giulio Binosi, a researcher specializing in hypercomplex analysis, harmonic analysis, and differential geometry. I work primarily with quaternionic and Clifford algebras, exploring their analytical and geometric properties.

Research Interests

  • Hypercomplex Analysis
  • Harmonic Analysis
  • Differential Geometry and Spinorial Methods
